Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

Everything you need to know about our air duct cleaning services, process, and pricing.

info General

Air duct cleaning is the process of removing dust, debris, allergens, mold, and other contaminants from your HVAC system's ductwork. This includes the supply and return air ducts, registers, grilles, diffusers, heat exchangers, cooling coils, and other components of your heating and cooling system.
The EPA recommends cleaning your air ducts every 3–5 years. However, you may need more frequent cleaning if you have pets, allergies, asthma, recent renovations, visible mold growth, or if you've recently moved into a new home. We recommend an annual inspection to assess your system's condition.
Common signs include: visible dust or debris around vents, musty or stale odors when the HVAC runs, increased allergy or asthma symptoms, uneven airflow between rooms, higher energy bills, or if it's been more than 5 years since your last cleaning.
